ɳClaw is a personal AI assistant with infinite, self-organizing memory. You just talk to it. It auto-detects topics, branches conversations, and builds a knowledge graph that compounds over time.

Your life, organized by topic — not by "New Chat".

Key features:

- Chat with your own AI model hosted on a server you control
- Automatic topic detection — conversations branch into focused threads
- Infinite memory that remembers facts, decisions, people, and preferences
- Voice conversations with speech-to-text and text-to-speech
- Share content from any app into your knowledge graph
- Home-screen widget for quick capture and recent conversations
- Morning briefings, daily digest, and proactive reminders
- Offline-first — keep working when the network is flaky
- End-to-end encryption with locally-held keys
- Connects to self-hosted nSelf backends — your data stays yours

ɳClaw is part of the nSelf ecosystem. Core is MIT-licensed open source. Premium features unlock via the ɳClaw plugin bundle.
